我们看到的机会是在这个锚点周围建立一个轻量、可逆的视觉层次 —— 它可以随每年的创意方向灵活变化，而绝不触碰核心标识。

Here's how we frame it:
我们是这样定位的：

- **The Anchor**: Core Diablo logo, permanent and untouched
- **锚点**: 暗黑破坏神核心LOGO，永久且不做改动

- **The Annual Expression**: A subtle thematic treatment applied to seasonal touchpoints (loading screens, key art, event branding) that reflects the year's overarching direction. Retired cleanly when the year concludes.
- **年度表达**: 一种 subtle 的主题性视觉处理，应用于季节性触点（加载画面、主视觉、活动品牌），反映全年的整体方向。当年结束时干净利落地退役。

For 2026, the expression draws from the Warlock launch as the year's key creative pillar.
2026年的表达从术士上线汲取灵感，作为当年的核心创意支柱。

For 2027, it would naturally shift to align with that year's broader directional push — whatever that may be.
2027年，它将自然转向与该年更广泛的 directional 推动对齐 —— 无论那是什么。

The intent is to give current and returning players a sense that the brand is alive and evolving, while the master logo remains the untouched, recognizable constant.
意图是给现有玩家和回流玩家一种品牌鲜活且持续演进的感觉，同时主LOGO保持不受触碰、可识别的恒定性。

This isn't about celebrating a single moment by changing the logo.
这不是通过改变LOGO来庆祝某个单一时刻。

It's about creating an annual rhythm where the seasonal layers do the storytelling, and the core mark stays exactly where it belongs.
而是创造一个年度节奏，让季节性层次承担叙事功能，核心标识始终留在它该在的位置。
