GUI设计小组希望借助这次新职业术士的上线，将Diablo的LOGO进行一些小小的变化，增加玩家新鲜度

The GUI Design Team would like to propose a subtle logo refresh timed with the upcoming Tempest class release, aiming to inject fresh visual interest for players.

同时我们将保持以下原则

Our guiding principles are as follows:

LOGO的整体不做过大的变动，而是让玩家感受到有小小更新和变化即可

Maintain the core Diablo Immortal logo structure — no radical redesign.

Deliver a "fresh but familiar" update that signals new content.

所以在设计上会保持克制但又透露一些小的新意想法

Exercise creative restraint while introducing meaningful new elements.

**Direction 1: Tempest Class-Inspired Concepts**

### 方案01

法阵火圈召唤魔物

Arcane circle + teleportation flame portal + summoned creatures

术士法阵+传送火圈+魔物

Capturing the Tempest's summoning mastery

### 方案02

术士眼，半开传送圈

The class's iconic eye motif, partially revealing a dimensional portal

### 方案03

Incorporating the class icon into a cutout dimensional rift design

### 方案04

术士的定稿设定以铁器为主，身上会附着类似禁忌符文的纹样

Reflecting the Tempest's finalized iron-heavy aesthetic with forbidden rune patterns

本方案主要将LOGO文字贴合术士要素为主要设计方向

This concept aligns the logo typography with the Tempest class identity

方案1更加传统，保持logo的体积感和周边边框

Option A (Traditional): Maintains logo volume and border integrity

Option B (Lightweight): Circular portal background featuring summoned entities
