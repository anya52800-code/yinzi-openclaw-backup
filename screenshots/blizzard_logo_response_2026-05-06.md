**主题**: 回复：LOGO更新讨论 —— 重新定义为年度主题性品牌表达

---

Hi [Name],
你好[Name]，

Thank you for the detailed feedback — it gave us a lot of clarity on the publishing team’s perspective, and we fully agree with the core principle:
感谢你详尽的反馈 —— 这让我们非常清楚地理解了发行团队的立场，我们完全认同这一核心原则：

**the Diablo logo should remain a permanent, long-lasting brand asset, not tied to temporary in-game moments.**
**暗黑破坏神LOGO应当保持为永久、持久的品牌资产，而非与游戏内的临时性事件挂钩。**

With that in mind, we’d like to reframe our proposal.
基于这一点，我们希望重新调整我们的提案。

Rather than a logo *change*, we’re proposing an **annual thematic brand expression system**:
与其称之为LOGO"更换"，我们提议建立一个**年度主题性品牌表达系统**：

**Core principle**: The master logo stays untouched. This is the anchor.
**核心原则**: 主LOGO保持不动。这是锚点。

What we’re suggesting is a lightweight, reversible visual layer that flexes around it each year — following a clearly defined annual theme, not reacting to individual content drops.
我们建议的是一个轻量、可逆的视觉层次，每年围绕主LOGO进行弹性变化 —— 遵循清晰定义的年度主题，而非针对单个内容更新做出反应。

Here’s how it would work:
具体运作方式如下：

- **Core Logo**: Permanent, never altered. This is the anchor.
- **核心LOGO**: 永久性，绝不改动。这是锚点。

- **Annual Thematic Expression**: A subtle, consistent visual treatment applied across key touchpoints (loading screens, seasonal key art, event branding) that reflects the year’s overarching creative direction.
- **年度主题表达**: 一种 subtle 且一致的视觉处理，应用于关键触点（加载画面、赛季主视觉、活动品牌物料），反映全年的整体创意方向。

For 2026, the theme is the Warlock class launch; for 2027, it would shift to whatever the broader directional push is.
2026年的主题是术士职业上线；2027年将转向当时更广泛的 directional 推动内容。

- **Reversibility**: These are not logo replacements. They’re seasonal brand layers that can be retired cleanly when the year/theme concludes, with the master logo always returning to its untouched state.
- **可逆性**: 这些并非LOGO替换。它们是季节性品牌层次，在当年/主题结束后可以干净地退役，主LOGO始终恢复其未触碰的原状。

This approach addresses the concern about tying a permanent brand element to a temporary event.
这一做法解决了将永久性品牌元素绑定到临时性事件的顾虑。

Warlock isn’t the *reason* to update the logo — it’s simply the first annual theme in a planned cadence.
术士并非更新LOGO的"理由" —— 它只是计划节奏中的第一个年度主题。

Next year, the expression shifts to match 2027’s direction, and so on.
明年，表达方式将转向匹配2027年的方向，以此类推。

We see this as building a living brand system that keeps the visual identity fresh for current and returning players, without ever diluting the core mark.
我们认为这是在构建一个有生命力的品牌系统，让现有玩家和回流玩家感受到视觉新鲜感，同时绝不稀释核心标识。

The publishing team would retain full approval rights over both the master logo and the annual thematic guidelines.
发行团队将保留对主LOGO和年度主题规范的双重审批权。

Does this direction feel more aligned with what you’re looking for?
这个方向是否更贴合你们的预期？

We’d love to sync on the 2027 roadmap soon so we can start planning the thematic framework together.
我们希望尽快对齐2027年的路线图，以便共同规划主题框架。

Let me know your thoughts!
期待你的想法！

Best,
此致，
